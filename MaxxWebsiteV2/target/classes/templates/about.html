About MAXX Energy

Pioneering the future of clean energy with innovative solar solutions and unmatched expertise since 2012.

Our Mission

To accelerate the global transition to renewable energy by delivering premium solar solutions that combine cutting-edge technology, exceptional craftsmanship, and unparalleled customer service.

At MAXX Energy, we believe that clean energy should be accessible, reliable, and profitable for everyone. Our team of certified professionals works tirelessly to design, install, and maintain solar systems that not only meet but exceed our customers' energy goals.

Our Story

Founded in 2012 by renewable energy veterans Freddy Lane and Mahmoud Elbayoumi, MAXX Energy emerged from a shared vision: making premium solar technology accessible to homeowners and businesses across the region.

What started as a small team of three passionate engineers has grown into the area's most trusted solar installer, with over 2,800 successful installations and counting.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition as a Top Solar Contractor by Solar Power World magazine for three consecutive years, and we maintain an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau.
